Unofficial Exp Calculator for DelugeRPG by Dmaster (beta ver.1 - 20200524)
Note: This is "Unofficial/Fan made Exp Calculator for DelugeRPG (up to 100M Exp) - A fork of Psyduck's Exp Calculator".
It assumes you have already beaten all regions and that your pokemon is at level 100.
You can battle any other trainers that give the same amount of exp as those in the suggested solution.
Usage: Enter your Current Exp and your Target Exp. If a solution is not automatically generated or Exp to target is not turn to 0, Add some exp from the trainer list on the right by clicking on them.
Thanks to "Mr.Clean" for gathering data about exp on each trainers and wild pokemon. | If you find bugs / errors, comment on this forum link ( https://f.pkmn.pw/6/16917)
